Holy Water

Description

Maybe one of your family memebers got possessed. Or you happened to make the lasagna dish and now the pasta demons won't leave your house, this recipe is for you

Ingredients
  • Your first born
  • A robe tassel from a priest
  • A low calorie soda
Cooking directions
  1. Sacrifice your first born, or middle child if first born was sacrified for lasagna
  2. Use your intimate knowledge of latin texts to begin the exorcism
  3. Boil off the low calorie soda to make a syrup
  4. This syrup is the real deal holy water. Clings to everything. Garnish with a robe tassel
